Privacy Policy

How we handle your information and protect your privacy

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide you with the best possible credit card advice experience:

Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: If you reach out to us via email, we'll have your email address and any other information you choose to share.
  • Feedback and Questions: Any questions, feedback, or experiences you share with us about credit cards.
  • Calculator Usage: When you use our annual fee calculator, we don't store any of your personal spending information.

Information We Don't Collect

  • Personal Financial Data: We never ask for or store your credit card numbers, bank account information, or detailed spending data.
  • Credit Reports: We don't access or store your credit reports or credit scores.
  • Social Security Numbers: We never request or store your SSN or other sensitive personal identifiers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide for these specific purposes:

  • Providing Advice: To answer your questions about credit cards and provide personalized recommendations.
  • Improving Our Service: To understand what information is most helpful and what we can explain better.
  • Communication: To respond to your emails and provide follow-up information when helpful.
  • Content Development: To identify topics we should cover or explain better on the site.

What We Don't Do

  • We don't sell your information to third parties
  • We don't use your information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent
  • We don't share your personal information with credit card companies or financial institutions
  • We don't use your information to make credit decisions or financial recommendations beyond general advice

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We're committed to keeping your information private and secure:

When We Might Share Information

  • Legal Requirements: Only if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.
  • Service Providers: We may use trusted third-party services (like email providers) that have access to the information you send us.
  • With Your Consent: Only if you explicitly ask us to share information with someone else.

While we protect your privacy, remember that any information you share publicly (like in public forums or social media) is not under our control.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information:

  • We use secure, encrypted connections (HTTPS) for all website interactions
  • We limit access to your information to only those who need it to help you
  • We regularly review and update our security practices
  • We don't store sensitive information on our servers

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your information:

  • Access: You can ask us what information we have about you.
  • Correction: You can ask us to correct any inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: You can ask us to delete your information (though this may limit our ability to help you).
  • Opt-out: You can stop receiving communications from us at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, simply email us at hello@cardsfornormals.com.

Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and tracking:

  • Essential Cookies: Basic cookies that make the website function properly.
  • Analytics: Basic analytics to understand how people use our site (no personal identification).
  • No Advertising: We don't use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

You can control cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you're a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We'll notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the bottom
  • Sending you an email if the changes significantly affect how we handle your information

Your continued use of our service after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

Contact Us
