Practical Recommendation

If JetBlue is your go-to a few times a year, this is the "easy mode" pick. You get a free first checked bag for you and up to three companions, a 5,000-point anniversary bonus, and a straightforward $100 JetBlue Vacations credit. The 10% points rebate on award flights is icing if you redeem even occasionally.

If you rarely fly JetBlue, the perks will sit unused. But for casual loyalists, one checked-bag round trip plus the Vacations credit can cover the fee—with room to spare. Bottom line: simple, solid value for JetBlue flyers who are not chasing premium lounges.

Perks

  • Earning structure (full detail) – 6x on JetBlue and Paisly purchases; 2x at restaurants; 2x at U.S. supermarkets; 1x everything else. Straightforward earn for everyday categories.
  • Free first checked bag (you + 3 companions) – On JetBlue-operated flights when you use the card as specified; good luggage savings for families.
  • Anniversary bonus – 5,000 TrueBlue points each account anniversary; handy offset to the annual fee.
  • 10% points back on award flights – Redeem TrueBlue points for a JetBlue-operated award and get 10% of the points back after you fly. Nice rebate if you redeem even once a year.
  • Inflight savings – 50% back on eligible food and drink purchases on JetBlue flights when you pay with the card.
  • Mosaic status progress via tiles – Earn 1 tile per $1,000 in card purchases toward JetBlue Mosaic status. Good slow-and-steady path if you put real spend on the card.
  • No foreign transaction fees – Use abroad without extra percentage fees.

Protections

  • Trip Cancellation/Interruption – Reimburses up to $5,000 per trip (max $10,000 per 12 months) for nonrefundable costs when a covered event (illness, severe weather, etc.) derails plans.
  • Trip Delay – If your common-carrier trip is delayed 6+ hours, get up to $300 per trip for lodging/meals/essentials (limits on number of claims per year).
  • Baggage Delay/Loss – Coverage for delayed bags (daily essentials) and lost/damaged luggage when the carrier is at fault; useful backup when travel goes sideways.
  • Auto Rental Collision Damage Waiver – Coverage for theft/collision when you pay with the card and decline the rental agency's waiver (vehicle/country exclusions apply).
  • Purchase Protection – Short-term coverage for damage/theft on eligible new items you buy with the card; handy for gadgets and small appliances.
