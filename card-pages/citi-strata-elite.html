Practical Recommendation

Hard to find much unique value here versus other premium travel cards. The credits are limited and fussy (one portal hotel discount, split Blacklane credit, niche "Splurge" brands), and the best earn rates live behind Citi's portal or a weekend dining window. If you do not already book through Citi Travel or spend with those brands, the $595 fee is tough to justify.

Who it fits: a narrow slice—AA flyers who'll actually use the four Admirals Club passes, already shop at a Splurge brand, and do not mind a portal hotel booking each year. Everyone else will likely get more value (with less micromanaging) from simpler premium options. Bottom line: outside the welcome bonus, most people will be better off elsewhere.

Perks

  • Earning rates (full detail) – 12x hotels, car rentals & attractions via cititravel.com; 6x air via cititravel.com; 6x restaurants on Friday & Saturday 6 p.m.–6 a.m. ET (Citi Nights); 3x restaurants other times; 1.5x everything else.
  • Priority Pass lounges – Complimentary Priority Pass Select for the primary cardmember (authorized users eligible) with up to 2 complimentary guests where allowed. No restaurants/experiences. Show your card or enroll for a digital pass. — See lounge list
  • American Airlines Admirals Club passes – Four digital passes per calendar year in your AAdvantage wallet. Each pass admits one adult (18+) plus up to three children under 18 for 24 hours, with same-day boarding pass on American or oneworld. Capacity controls apply; some clubs turn away day-pass users at peak times. — See lounge list
  • The Reserve by Citi Travel (hotel collection) – Book select 4.5–5-star properties via Citi Travel for perks like breakfast for two, property credit, and potential upgrade/late checkout; always compare rates. — See details
  • Points transfer – Transfer ThankYou points to partners including American Airlines, JetBlue, Virgin Atlantic, Cathay Pacific, EVA Air, Choice Hotels, and The Leading Hotels of the World. Good value if you can find saver awards.
  • No foreign transaction fees – Use abroad without the extra skim.

Protections

  • Trip Delay Insurance – Helps cover meals and hotels when a covered trip is significantly delayed; useful for weather cancellations or missed connections.
  • Trip Cancellation & Interruption (common carrier) – Reimburses prepaid, non-refundable trip costs when you must cancel or cut a trip short for covered reasons.
  • Lost or Damaged Luggage – Pays toward repairing or replacing baggage and contents when a carrier loses or damages your stuff.
  • Rental Car Insurance (MasterRental) – Coverage for theft/damage when you pay with the card and decline the rental agency's collision damage waiver.
  • Purchase Protection (Purchase Assurance Plus) – Short-term coverage for theft or accidental damage on eligible new items.
  • Extended Warranty – Extends eligible U.S. manufacturer warranties on covered purchases.
  • Travel Medical & Assistance (MasterAssist) – Access to medical assistance coordination and certain travel services; you pay the actual service costs.
