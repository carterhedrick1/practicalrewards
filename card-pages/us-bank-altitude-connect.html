Practical Recommendation

Altitude Connect is a rare no-fee travel card with real multipliers: 4x on broad travel and gas, plus 5x on hotels and car rentals if you book through the bank's portal. Redemptions are simple (cash or travel), and you get a small taste of premium perks without paying a premium fee.

If you're fine booking portal hotels to chase 5x and you'll actually use the four Priority Pass lounge visits and the TSA PreCheck or Global Entry credit, this is easy value. If you avoid portals on principle, treat it like a solid 4x travel/4x gas card and call it a day.

Perks

  • Earning, at a glance – 5x on prepaid hotels and car rentals via the Altitude Rewards Center; 4x on travel; 4x on gas and ev charging (first $1,000 per quarter); 2x on dining, streaming, groceries; 1x on everything else. Use the portal when prices are equal or better; otherwise earn 4x on travel booked direct.
  • Priority Pass lounges – Complimentary Priority Pass Select membership with 4 free visits per membership year (enrollment required). Extra visits are paid. Good for occasional trips where a lounge makes delays less annoying. — See lounge list
  • No foreign transaction fees – Useful for international trips and foreign merchants; avoids the 1–3% many cards still charge.
  • Real-Time Rewards – Optional text-to-redeem setup that lets you use points as a statement credit on eligible purchases after you make them. Handy if you like instant, simple redemptions.
  • GigSky global mobile data – Complimentary data plans in many countries (enrollment needed; offer windows apply). Good backup when Wi-Fi fails on trips. — GigSky enrollment

Protections

  • Trip cancellation/interruption – Reimburses prepaid, non-refundable travel expenses for covered reasons when you paid with the card. Helps when you need to bail on a trip for qualifying events. — See your Guide to Benefits
  • Trip delay reimbursement – Covers reasonable meals and lodging when a covered trip is delayed beyond the required threshold. Useful for weather or missed connections. — See your Guide to Benefits
  • SmartDelay – Register flights and receive lounge access vouchers if your flight meets the delay trigger. Good "plan B" when things go sideways. — Register here
  • Rental car insurance (secondary CDW) – Pays for damage or theft to the rental car when you pay with the card; secondary to your personal auto policy; not liability coverage. — See your Guide to Benefits
  • Purchase protection & extended warranty – Helps with theft/accidental damage shortly after purchase and can extend eligible manufacturer warranties. Can save you from buying extra coverage on gadgets. — See your Guide to Benefits
