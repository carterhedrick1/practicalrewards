Practical Recommendation

If you can use the annual 40,000-point free night and occasionally book 4-night award stays, this is an "easy keeper." Platinum Elite is automatic, and stacking card + member + status points makes paid IHG stays rack up fast.

If you never set foot in an IHG hotel, skip it. But for casual IHG guests who can aim one night per year at a solid property, the fee is tiny compared to the value.

United TravelBank Cash
What It Is: $50 per calendar year as two $25 United TravelBank deposits (Jan and Jul) after one-time registration.
How It Works: Link your IHG and United accounts at ihg.com/united; deposits post to your MileagePlus TravelBank and are auto-applied when you choose TravelBank at checkout on United/United Express flights.
Gotchas: Each $25 deposit expires fast (Jan deposit expires Jul 15; Jul deposit expires Jan 15). No transfers, no extensions, United-operated flights only. Registration required; allow up to ~2 weeks for first deposit.
Who Actually Benefits: Flyers who book at least one United ticket every six months.
Practical Value: Real $50 if you fly United sometimes; $0 if you forget the short expiry.

Perks

  • Earning rates (detail) – 10x at ihg hotels; 5x on travel, dining, and gas; 3x on everything else. Strong earn on IHG stays and common travel/food.
  • Platinum Elite status (automatic) – As long as you hold the card. Useful for upgrades, bonus points, and late checkout where available. — See status
  • Anniversary Free Night (up to 40,000 points) – One certificate every account anniversary; you can top-off with points to reach a higher-priced night. — Free Night rules
  • Fourth Night Free on award stays – Book 4+ consecutive award nights and every 4th night costs 0 points. Great for long weekends and week-long stays. — Benefit details
  • Path to Diamond – Spend $40,000 in a calendar year to earn Diamond Elite through Dec 31 of the following year.
  • Save 20% on purchased points – When buying points with this card via IHG/Points.com.
  • No foreign transaction fees – Use abroad without the extra percentage markup.

Protections

  • Trip Cancellation/Interruption – Up to $5,000 per covered traveler / $10,000 per trip for prepaid, non-refundable expenses when trips are canceled/shortened for covered reasons.
  • Baggage Delay – $100 per day up to 3 days for essentials when bags are delayed 6+ hours.
  • Lost Luggage – Up to $3,000 per covered traveler if bags are lost, stolen, or damaged.
  • Purchase Protection – Eligible new purchases covered against damage or theft for 120 days, up to $500 per item.
  • Travel Accident Insurance & Travel/Emergency Assistance – AD&D coverage on eligible common-carrier tickets; referral help for emergencies while traveling.
