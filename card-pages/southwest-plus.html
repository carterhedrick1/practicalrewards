Practical Recommendation

This is the simple Southwest card that now comes with real perks: a free first checked bag for you and up to eight companions, a 10% off flight code each year (excludes Basic fares), and 3,000 anniversary points. Add Group 5 boarding and standard seat selection within 48 hours (for 2026 flights), and it's easy day-to-day value.

If you fly Southwest even once a year, bag savings plus the 10% code can wipe out the annual fee. If you rarely touch Southwest, skip it. Bottom line: easy keeper for casual Southwest flyers who want benefits without fuss.

Perks

  • Earning (full detail) – 2x on Southwest purchases; 2x at gas stations & grocery stores on the first $5,000 per anniversary year; plus through Dec 31, 2025: 2x with Rapid Rewards hotel & car partners, local transit & commuting (incl. rideshare), and internet/cable/phone & select streaming.
  • First checked bag free (you + up to 8 companions) – Add your Rapid Rewards number; savings add up quickly on a single round trip.
  • Group 5 boarding – You and up to eight on the same reservation board with Group 5 (earlier overhead-bin access).
  • Seat selection (2026 flights) – Standard seat selection within 48 hours of departure when available (assigned seating begins for flights departing Jan 27, 2026).
  • Anniversary & status help – 3,000 anniversary points each year; 10,000 Companion Pass qualifying points boost annually; plus 1,500 tier-qualifying points per $5,000 in card spend.
  • 10% flight promo code (each anniversary year) – Single code deposited to your Rapid Rewards account; excludes Basic fares.
  • 25% back inflight – Automatic 25% statement credit on eligible inflight purchases when you pay with the card.
  • No foreign transaction fees – Use abroad without extra percentage fees.

Protections

  • Lost Luggage Reimbursement – Up to $3,000 per covered traveler if the carrier loses or damages your bags.
  • Baggage Delay Insurance – Up to $100/day for 3 days for essentials if your bag is delayed more than 6 hours.
  • Purchase Protection – Covers eligible new items for 120 days against damage/theft, up to $500 per item.
  • Extended Warranty – Adds 1 year on eligible U.S. warranties of 3 years or less (max 4 years total).
