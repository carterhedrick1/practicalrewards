Practical Recommendation

If you live on DoorDash, this is the easy pick: 4% on-app, 3% direct dining, 2% groceries, and a free year of DashPass that starts right away. No annual fee and no foreign transaction fees keep it simple.

After year one, you can earn another free year of DashPass by putting $10,000 on the card each anniversary year. If you do not order often or will not hit the spend, park this for DoorDash orders only and use a stronger everyday earner for the rest.

Perks

  • Earning rates (detailed) – 4% on DoorDash and Caviar orders; 3% on dining purchased directly from restaurants; 2% at grocery stores (online or in-store); 1% on everything else. Rewards are tracked as points and redeem as cash back.
  • DashPass (12 months on approval) – Complimentary DashPass for the primary cardholder (and up to one authorized user) starts upon approval/activation; auto-renews at the then-current annual price unless you cancel. Use the card at checkout to get DashPass benefits.
  • DashPass Anniversary bonus – Spend $10,000 in an anniversary year to get another 12 months of DashPass. If you do not qualify, your membership continues at the standard annual rate unless you cancel.
  • 10% Monthly Non-Restaurant Order Discount (through Dec 31, 2025) – Once per calendar month, get 10% off one convenience, grocery, alcohol, retail, or DashMart order (max $15 discount) when you pay with this card in DoorDash. It is a checkout promo, not points. Gotchas: only one order per month; non-restaurant category only; fees, taxes, and tip still apply; availability varies; DoorDash can change or end it. Practical value: easy savings if you place even one qualifying order each month.
  • Flexible redemptions – Use rewards to pay DoorDash/Caviar orders at checkout, or redeem for a statement credit, direct deposit, or gift cards.
  • No foreign transaction fees – Good for travel or international merchants; the fee is 0%.

Protections

  • Purchase Protection – Coverage for theft or damage on eligible new purchases for a limited time after purchase; caps and exclusions apply (check your Guide to Benefits).
  • Extended Warranty – Adds extra time to eligible U.S. manufacturer warranties (limits apply).
  • Auto Rental Collision Damage Waiver – Coverage for damage/theft when you decline the rental company's collision waiver and pay with the card; liability not included; country/vehicle restrictions apply.
