Practical Recommendation

Want no-annual-fee travel points without homework? This is it. You get 1.5x on literally everything and 3x when you book travel through the bank's portal, then redeem points at a clean 1¢ each for travel or dining statement credits.

If you bank with Bank of America, Preferred Rewards quietly boosts earnings by 25%–75% and makes this punch above its weight. If you need lounges or chunky credits, look elsewhere. Bottom line: simple, flexible, and easy to keep long term.

Perks

  • Earning, at a glance – 1.5x on all purchases with no caps; 3x on travel booked through the Bank of America Travel Center. Use the portal when prices match or beat booking direct.
  • Preferred Rewards boost (25%–75%) – Enroll to get an automatic bonus on base earn; turns 1.5x into up to 2.625x and portal 3x into up to 5.25x if you qualify.
  • Redemptions that are simple – Redeem for statement credits toward travel or restaurant purchases at 1¢ per point (travel/dining), or as cash into eligible Bank of America/Merrill accounts. Redemptions typically start at 2,500 points.
  • No foreign transaction fees – Safe pick for international trips and foreign merchants; no 1%–3% surcharge.
  • Introductory interest offer – Often includes a 0% intro period on purchases; good for planned expenses you can pay off before interest.

Protections

  • Rental car insurance (secondary collision damage waiver) – Covers theft/damage to the rental car when you pay with the card and decline the agency's coverage; typically secondary in the U.S.
  • Lost luggage reimbursement – Helps when a carrier misplaces your bags; caps and exclusions apply.
  • Extended warranty – Adds extra warranty time on eligible purchases; useful on electronics and appliances.
  • Purchase security – Short window of coverage for theft or accidental damage on eligible new items.
  • Travel & emergency assistance; concierge – 24/7 help services (not insurance).
