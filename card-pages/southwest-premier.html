Practical Recommendation

If Southwest is your go-to a few times a year, this card is an easy keeper: 6,000 anniversary points, two EarlyBird Check-Ins reimbursed, Group 5 boarding, and a 15% flight promo code each year. Those alone can offset the annual fee with one planned trip.

You also get a yearly 10,000-point boost toward Companion Pass and can earn tier-qualifying points from spend. If you never book Southwest or do not want to use EarlyBird or the promo code, skip it. Bottom line: best for Southwest regulars who like simple, guaranteed value.

Handy if you already use DoorDash; $0 if you do not activate.

Perks

  • Earning structure (full detail) – 3x southwest purchases (plus 3x southwest cruises through Dec 31, 2025); 2x restaurants & grocery stores on first $8,000 per anniversary year; 2x rapid rewards hotel & car partners; 2x local transit & commuting (incl. rideshare); 2x internet, cable, phone & select streaming (the 2x partner/category boosts run through Dec 31, 2025); 1x everything else. Simple everyday coverage for Southwest fans.
  • First checked bag free (cardmember + up to 8 on same reservation) – Useful now that Southwest has new fare types; just add your Rapid Rewards number at booking.
  • Preferred seat selection (within 48 hours, when available) – Choose a Preferred or Standard seat for you and up to 8 companions on the same reservation. Great for families who want to sit together without sprinting at check-in.
  • Group 5 boarding – Board earlier than general boarding for better overhead space; extends to up to 8 companions on the same reservation.
  • Anniversary & status help – 6,000 anniversary points each year; 10,000 Companion Pass qualifying points boost annually; earn 1,500 tier-qualifying points per $5,000 in purchases to push toward A-List.
  • 15% flight promo code (each anniversary year) – One code deposited to your Rapid Rewards account (excludes Basic fares). Easy way to knock down cash fare once a year.
  • 25% back inflight – Automatic 25% statement credit on drinks and Wi-Fi purchased onboard with the card.
  • No foreign transaction fees – Use abroad without extra percentage fees.

Protections

  • Lost Luggage Reimbursement – Up to $3,000 per covered traveler for checked or carry-on bags lost, damaged, or stolen by the carrier. Read the guide for New York-specific caps.
  • Baggage Delay Insurance – Up to $100 per day for 3 days for essentials if your bag is delayed over 6 hours. Handy for toiletries and a shirt when your bag takes a detour.
  • Purchase Protection – Covers eligible new purchases for 120 days against damage or theft, up to $500 per item.
  • Extended Warranty – Adds 1 extra year on U.S. manufacturer warranties of 3 years or less (max 4 years total).
