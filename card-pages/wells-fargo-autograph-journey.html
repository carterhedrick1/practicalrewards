Perks

  • High Earning rates – 5x hotels • 4x airlines • 3x dining & other travel (rideshare, transit, car rentals, cruises) • 1x everything else. Put trip spend and eating out here to rack up points quickly.
  • Airline & Hotel Transfer Partners – You can move points to several airline and hotel programs for potentially higher value redemptions (most 1:1; Choice Privileges 1:2). Handy if you like award flights or hotel stays. Check the current partner list before you transfer—partners can change.
  • No Foreign Transaction Fees – Useful for international trips; your purchases abroad are not dinged by extra percentage fees.
  • Cell Phone Protection – Pay your monthly phone bill with the card and you're covered if your phone is damaged or stolen (up to $1,000 per claim with a $25 deductible). Good for families with multiple lines.

Protections

  • Trip Cancellation & Interruption – If a covered reason (like illness or severe weather) nukes your trip, you can be reimbursed for non-refundable costs such as flights or prepaid hotels. Helpful for big trips you'd hate to eat. (See issuer terms for covered reasons/limits.)
  • Trip Delay Reimbursement – Stuck due to a significant delay? Covered incidental expenses like meals or a hotel night may be reimbursed when the delay meets the stated threshold. Handy for missed connections and weather messes. (Review the guide for time and dollar caps.)
  • Lost Luggage Reimbursement – If your checked or carry-on bag is lost or damaged by a carrier, you may be reimbursed up to the program limit. Good peace of mind for checked-bag travelers.
  • Rental Car Collision Damage Waiver – Decline the rental company's collision damage waiver and pay with the card; damage or theft of the vehicle is covered up to $50,000 (liability not included). Saves you from buying overpriced counter insurance.
  • Roadside Dispatch & Travel/Emergency Assistance – Access a pay-per-use roadside hotline and a 24/7 travel help desk to find services in a pinch. Useful when you need a tow, a locksmith, or help while abroad.
