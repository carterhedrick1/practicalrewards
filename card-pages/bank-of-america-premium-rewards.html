Practical Recommendation

Want a simple travel card that actually pays for itself? This is it. You get clean earn rates (2x on travel and dining, 1.5x everywhere else), easy redemptions at a flat 1¢ per point, and two useful credits that can wipe out the annual fee if you fly even once a year.

It's good out of the box; it's great if you bank with Bank of America. Enroll in Preferred Rewards and the 25%–75% boost quietly turns 2x into up to 3.5x and 1.5x into up to 2.625x. If you want transfer partners or lounges, look elsewhere. If you want easy value, this is low-maintenance.

Perks

  • Simple redemptions at 1¢ – Redeem points for statement credits, travel through the portal, or cash deposits; practical value is a clean 1¢ per point. — Redemption overview

Protections

  • Trip Delay Insurance – Reimburses up to $500 for hotels/meals when a covered trip is delayed 12+ hours. Useful for overnight disruptions.
  • Trip Cancellation/Interruption – Up to $2,500 for nonrefundable passenger fares if you must cancel/cut short for covered reasons.
  • Baggage Delay/Loss – Essentials reimbursement for delayed bags and coverage for lost items by the carrier (caps apply).
  • Rental Car Insurance (Secondary CDW) – Covers theft/damage to your rental when you pay with the card and decline the agency's CDW (not liability).
  • Purchase Protection – Short-term coverage for theft/accidental damage on eligible new items.
  • Extended Warranty – Adds extra warranty time on eligible purchases.

Preferred Rewards: How to Boost Your Points

If you bank with Bank of America or Merrill, you can boost your points by 25% to 75%. Here's how it works:

How to Qualify

Step 1: Open a Bank of America checking account (required)

Step 2: Maintain qualifying balances across your accounts:

  • What counts: Any mix of Bank of America deposits + Merrill accounts (IRAs and 529s included)
  • How to qualify: Keep the tier's 3-month average, or get early enrollment by hitting the balance within 30 days of opening your checking account

What It Does to This Card

Preferred Rewards boosts your earned points by:

  • Gold: +25% boost
  • Platinum: +50% boost
  • Platinum Honors: +75% boost (maximum for credit cards)

The boost applies to your earned points after purchase, turning 2x travel/dining into up to 3.5x and 1.5x everything else into up to 2.625x.
