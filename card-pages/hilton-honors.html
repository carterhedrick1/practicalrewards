Practical Recommendation

If you touch Hilton a couple times a year, this is the simple, no-fee way to collect points: 7x on Hilton stays, 5x on common U.S. categories, and built-in Silver status (unlocks the fifth night free on awards). Pair it with a flexible travel card for protections and you're set.

Do not expect premium perks—no annual free night or credits here. Bottom line: great starter or drawer card for light Hilton users; frequent Hilton travelers should look at Surpass or Aspire for richer perks.

Perks

  • Earning details (plain English) – 7x at hilton hotels & resorts; 5x at U.S. restaurants, U.S. supermarkets, and U.S. gas stations; 3x on everything else. — Earning rates (Amex overview)
  • Silver status included; path to Gold – Complimentary Hilton Honors Silver while you hold the card; Gold after $20,000 in purchases in a calendar year (through end of the next year). — Status terms — Hilton cards page
  • Fifth Night Free on award stays – With elite status (Silver or higher), every 5th night on a standard room award is free (up to 20 nights). Great for longer points stays. — How it works
  • No foreign transaction fees – Use it abroad without extra Amex fees; merchant acceptance still matters. — No FX fee detail
  • Made with recycled material – Card body uses 70% reclaimed plastic (new design). Small win for the planet. — Hilton press note

Protections

  • Car Rental Loss & Damage Insurance (secondary) – Coverage for theft or damage when you pay with the card and decline the rental agency's collision waiver; liability not included; geographic and vehicle exclusions apply. — Benefit guide hub
  • Purchase Protection – Covers eligible new purchases against theft or accidental damage for 90 days, up to $1,000 per covered purchase and $50,000 per account per year. — Purchase protection terms
  • Extended Warranty – Adds up to 1 extra year on eligible U.S. manufacturer warranties of 5 years or less (limits and exclusions apply). — Extended warranty terms
