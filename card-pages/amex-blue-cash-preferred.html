Practical Recommendation

Want outsized cash back without juggling a dozen credits? This is the classic "groceries + streaming" play. If your supermarket bill is decent, 6% can erase the fee quickly; the broad 3% on gas and transit fills in the rest.

Bottom line: If your cart and queue are always full, this is easy, reliable value. Light spenders or warehouse-club shoppers should skip it.
