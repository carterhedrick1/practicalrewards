Practical Recommendation

Venture is the "set it and forget it" travel card: 2x on everything, with easy 5x when you book hotels or cars through the portal. Redemptions are flexible—erase travel charges, book in the portal, or transfer to airline and hotel partners.

If you travel more than a couple times a year, consider Venture X instead: the $300 Capital One Travel credit and 10,000 anniversary miles can offset most (or all) of its $395 fee, plus you get lounge access. Bottom line: Venture is simple; Venture X is the better traveler deal if you'll use the credits.
