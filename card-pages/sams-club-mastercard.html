Practical Recommendation

Great if you already shop at Sam's and especially if you're a Plus member. Aim the card at gas (easy 5%), dining (3%), and your Sam's runs (3% with Plus). Rewards post as Sam's Cash you can spend in-club/online or cash out at the desk.

If you are not Plus, you only get 1% at Sam's, so treat this like a gas-and-dining card and keep a simple 2% card for everything else.

Perks

  • Earning rates (detailed) – 5% at U.S. gas stations and EV charging stations (and Sam's/Walmart fuel) up to $6,000 per calendar year, then 1%; 3% at restaurants/takeout; 3% at Sam's Club for Plus members (Club members earn 1%); 1% on other purchases.
  • Plus stacking to "up to 5%" in-club – Plus membership adds 2% Sam's Cash on qualifying in-club purchases (capped at $500/year), stacking with the card's 3% for a total up to 5%.
  • Sam's Cash redemption – Sam's Cash loads to your membership monthly; redeem for most Sam's purchases or cash out at the club. Simple and flexible.
  • Annual Sam's Cash cap – Cardholders can earn up to $5,000 in Sam's Cash from the credit card in a calendar year; separate from the Plus 2% cap.
  • No foreign transaction fees – Useful when traveling; issuer fee is 0%.
  • Doubles as your membership card – The physical card also works as your Sam's membership ID for entry and checkout.

Protections

  • Mastercard protections – Includes Zero Liability and ID Theft Protection; details vary by benefit level—see your Guide to Benefits.
