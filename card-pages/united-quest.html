Practical Recommendation

If you fly United a few times a year, this is the "set it and forget it" pick: two free checked bags for you and a companion, early boarding, and an automatic $200 in United TravelBank each year. Add the simple 2x on travel and dining and you are already close to break-even.

The extra award-flight discounts (10,000 miles at each anniversary and another 10,000 miles after $20,000 in annual spend) plus the ability to earn Premier qualifying points (status points) via spend are nice—but only if you'll use them. Bottom line: United flyers who value free bags and a guaranteed $200 flight offset will be happy here.

Credits

Practical Advice

This is what makes or breaks a card for you. If you can use these credits in your normal spend, the perks and protections are all bonus for you. We have a calculator at the bottom of this section to help you calculate that. Below we break down exactly what every credit is to show you if it's easy or if they're going to make you jump through 17 hoops to redeem it.

  • $200 United TravelBank Credit – Once after account opening and every account anniversary, $200 in United TravelBank cash is deposited to your MileagePlus account for United or United Express flights. Valid 12 months from issue; use on united.com/app; cannot combine with travel certificates or Money + Miles. Good if you buy even one United ticket a year.
  • Renowned Hotels and Resorts Credit (up to $150/yr) – Up to $150 back in statement credits each anniversary year when you prepay hotel stays through Renowned Hotels and Resorts for United cardmembers. Prices/selection differ from booking direct; benefits like breakfast and credits are property-specific. Worth it if you'll actually use the program.
  • Rideshare Credit (up to $100/yr) – Enroll, then get up to $8 back per month Jan–Nov and up to $12 back in Dec on rideshare purchases paid with the card. Yearly opt-in required; unused monthly amounts do not roll. Easy value if you ride monthly; $0 if you do not enroll.
  • Avis/Budget Credit (up to $80/yr) – Get $40 in United TravelBank cash for each of your first two Avis or Budget rentals per anniversary year when booked via cars.united.com and paid with the card. TravelBank rules apply; third-party bookings do not qualify. Nice if you rent twice.
  • Instacart Credits (up to $180/yr, through 12/31/27) – Monthly $10 + $5 Instacart credits when you pay directly at Instacart with the card (plus a 3-month Instacart+ trial, then 50% off annual). Service fees apply; offer ends 12/31/27. Great if you already use Instacart; otherwise skip.
  • JSX Credit (up to $150/yr) – Up to $150 back each anniversary year on flights purchased direct from JSX with the card. Not everyone lives near JSX; strong if you do.
  • Trusted Traveler Credit (up to $120 every 4 years) – Statement credit for Global Entry, TSA PreCheck, or NEXUS application fee when charged to the card. Pick Global Entry if you travel abroad; it includes TSA PreCheck eligibility.

Perks

  • Earning structure (full detail) – 3x united purchases; 2x travel (airfare, hotels, transit, rideshare, tolls, cruises, car rentals); 2x dining; 2x select streaming; 1x everything else; 5x on prepaid hotels via Renowned Hotels and Resorts for United cardmembers. Good everyday travel coverage plus a niche hotel multiplier.
  • Free first & second checked bags – Primary cardmember + 1 companion on the same reservation get two bags each on United-operated flights (buy the ticket with the card; waived purchase requirement for first 90 days). Can save up to $360 round-trip.
  • Priority boarding – Board before general boarding for easier overhead bin space. Applies to the cardmember and companions on the same reservation.
  • Award flight discounts – 10,000-mile discount deposited after each account anniversary; another 10,000-mile discount after $20,000 in calendar-year spend. Helpful if you redeem miles annually.
  • Premier qualifying points via spend – Earn 1 Premier qualifying point (PQP) for every $20 in purchases, up to 18,000 PQP per year; starting 2026, 1,000 "Card Bonus" PQP post annually. Useful for nudging toward United Premier status.
  • In-flight savings – Get 25% back on United-operated onboard purchases (food, drinks, Wi-Fi) and premium drink purchases in United Club locations when you pay with the card.
  • Complimentary Premier upgrades on award tickets – If you already have United Premier status, you and a companion are eligible for complimentary upgrades on award flights, when available.
  • No foreign transaction fees – Use abroad without extra percentage fees.

Protections

  • Trip Cancellation/Interruption – Reimburses up to $1,500 per traveler and $6,000 per trip for nonrefundable fares when a covered reason (illness, severe weather, etc.) forces you to cancel or cut a trip short.
  • Trip Delay – If a covered trip is delayed 12+ hours or requires an overnight stay, get up to $500 per traveler for lodging/meals/essentials.
  • Baggage Delay – Up to $100 per day for 3 days for essentials when baggage is delayed 6+ hours.
  • Lost/Damaged Luggage – Up to $3,000 per traveler for checked or carry-on baggage lost or damaged by the carrier.
  • Auto Rental Collision Damage Waiver (primary) – Primary coverage up to $60,000 for theft/collision when you pay with the card and decline the rental company's waiver (vehicle and country exclusions apply).
  • Purchase Protection – Covers eligible new items against damage/theft for 120 days, up to $10,000 per item.
  • Extended Warranty – Adds 1 additional year on eligible U.S. manufacturer warranties of 3 years or less (max 4 years total from purchase).
